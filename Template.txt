ITEM META DATA:

    shirt|<defence>|c|i
    trouser|<defence>|c|i
    cloak|<defence>|c|i
    book|<knowledge/book name>|i
    map|<map name>|i
    weapon|<damage>|i
    money|<amount>|i

    types:
        i = non-usable item
        c = clothing/equipable item
        e = equipped item
        p = place

        building|<type>
